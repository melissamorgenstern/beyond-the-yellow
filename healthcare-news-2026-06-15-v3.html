1. Medicare Trust Fund Insolvency Moves One Quarter Closer — ‘Big Beautiful Bill’ Tax Cuts Hit Home

The 2026 Medicare Trustees Report (June 9) projects Part A trust fund depletion in Q2 2033 — one quarter earlier than last year’s estimate. For Zocdoc, this means growing uncertainty around provider payment rates, potential network churn as MA enrollment passes 51%, and new patient demand from the GLP-1 benefit.

3. CMS Creates Office of Health Technology — Federal Bet on AI, Interoperability, and Digital Products

On June 10, CMS established the Office of Health Technology and Products (OHTP), led by Amy Gleason as deputy administrator and chief product officer. Eight operating groups cover AI implementation, interoperability standards, product development, and the National Provider Directory. The office will also oversee open-source frameworks and Medicare claims modernization. For Zocdoc, federal investment in provider directories and interoperability could create new data integration opportunities — or new compliance standards — for marketplace platforms that connect patients to providers.

Medicare Rights Center / CMS · June 4, 2026
Starting July 1, eligible Medicare Part D enrollees can access Wegovy, Zepbound, and Foundayo for a $50/month copay through the Medicare GLP-1 Bridge program, running through December 2027. Zocdoc relevance: A new covered benefit for 70M beneficiaries will drive demand for weight management providers, endocrinologists, and primary care physicians who can prescribe GLP-1s — a searchable, bookable category on Zocdoc.
